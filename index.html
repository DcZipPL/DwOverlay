Free and Open Source desktop organization program

Orgianize your desktop.

With Tiels you can change your ugly and uncatorized desktop. Icons in Tiels can be left clicked, right clicked and sorted*.
Roll and unroll tiels whenever you want.

* "Sort" feature not implemented yet!
Customize Tiels!

You can change background colors of your tiels, toggle blur and change text color. In feature updates you can set image as background.
